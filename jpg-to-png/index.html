‚ùì FAQ: JPG to PNG Conversion

Will I lose quality when converting JPG to PNG?

No. While JPG is lossy, PNG is a **lossless format**. Our tool preserves the original pixels of your JPG during the transition.

What is the 256-color (UPNG) option?

It is an advanced optimization that shrinks PNG file sizes by limiting the palette to 256 colors. It keeps the image looking sharp while making it much lighter for web use.

Why can't I upload more than 20 files?

To ensure **maximum speed** and prevent browser crashes on mobile, we've optimized the batch limit to 20 files per session.
