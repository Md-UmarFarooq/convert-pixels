Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ConvertPixels, we believe in transparent, privacy-focused image processing. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our web application.

Core Principle: ConvertPixels processes all images locally in your browser. We have no servers that receive, store, or process your image files.

100% Local Processing

Your files are processed locally using your browser's hardware. When you upload images to ConvertPixels, they never leave your computer. All conversion, resizing, and formatting operations happen entirely within your browser's memory using JavaScript and WebAssembly technologies.

We have no servers that store your image data. Our application runs client-side only. We don't have image processing servers, cloud storage, or databases that could hold your files.

Data Collection & Tracking

Zero Data Collection: ConvertPixels does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your original or converted image files
  • Image metadata (EXIF data, GPS coordinates, etc.)
  • Filenames or directory structures
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage patterns or browsing history

We do not log filenames, metadata, or IP addresses associated with your files. Our application has no analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry systems that monitor your usage.

No Cookies: ConvertPixels does not use cookies, local storage, or session storage to track users. Any temporary browser storage is used solely for the current conversion session and is cleared when you close the tab.

Large-Scale Processing Safety

Large-scale processing (up to 10K resolution) happens entirely in your RAM. When working with high-resolution images, all processing occurs within your device's memory. This ensures that even temporary file states don't get written to disk where they could potentially be recovered.

The browser's sandboxed environment provides additional security layers, ensuring that image data cannot access other browser tabs or system resources without explicit permission.

Third-Party Services

ConvertPixels does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The application is completely self-contained within your browser.

Our website is served through standard hosting infrastructure, which may log basic connection data (as all websites do), but these logs are not connected to your image processing activities in any way.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ConvertPixels after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
